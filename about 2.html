About Porkyto's Restaurant

Your favorite spot for delicious dishes.

Our Story

Porkyto's restaurant was established in 2020 with a simple mission: to bring the best-tasting pork dishes to our community. We started as a small family-owned business, and thanks to our loyal customers, we've grown into a beloved local eatery.

Special Events

We love to celebrate with our customers! Join us for our special events:

  • Weekend Grill Fest: Every Saturday and Sunday, we fire up the grill for an all-day feast.
  • Spicy Kebab Night: Every Friday, we feature our famous spicy kebabs at a special price.

Meet the Owner

MR CHARLES APPIAH

MR Charles, the founder and head chef of Porkyto's rstaurant, has over 20 years of experience in the culinary arts. His passion for pork and his dedication to quality have made Porkyto's a local favorite.
